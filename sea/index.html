CALL OF THE SEA

ABOUT

A cross-century milestone, symbolizing the abundance of life and nurturing Taiwanese culture with the ocean...

Combining aquariums and fully digital imaging methods, the "World Waters Pavilion" covering global waters and ancient oceans is introduced. Through the integration of cutting-edge technology, it displays the four major themes of ancient oceans, seaweed forests, deep waters, and polar waters. It enables the visiting people to achieve an entertaining and educational experience in the creation of a combination of virtual and physical situations. The "atlantis" will not only become the world's premier museum, but will also write the most dazzling chapter for the Republic of China in cross-century marine education, research, and display.

our mission

A cross-century milestone, symbolizing the abundance of life and nurturing Taiwanese culture with the ocean...

Carry out first-class and unique research in marine biology and related sciences. Promote popular science education with research, faithfully present the mysterious underwater world with accurate biological display; enrich the collection of marine biological entities and digital information, and build a large marine biological library. Stimulate the creativity of production, education and research, transform and export technology and cultural creativity. The Pangze community benefits the country and ranks among the international ranks.
